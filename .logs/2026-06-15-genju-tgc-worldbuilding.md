# Genju TGC worldbuilding

Time: 2026-06-15

Goal: define the world premise and base rules for a fantasy TGC RPG built around genju, contract cards, and a 6x6 chess-like summon board.

Outcome:
- Established the protagonist as a traveler from a genju protection and research guild.
- Established contract cards, magical projection summons, and regional value conflicts around genju.
- Defined the initial TGC rules: 30-card deck, 6x6 board, 20 life, mana growth, card classes, rarity constraints, summon rules, movement, combat, equipment, and starter deck ratio.
- Wrote the design spec at `docs/superpowers/specs/2026-06-15-genju-tgc-world-design.md`.
